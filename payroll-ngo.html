THE CHALLENGE

Payroll Fraud in Non-Profits

Unfortunately, money has been stolen or misappropriated in many instances through payroll fraud. This undermines the noble work of non-profit organizations and erodes donor trust.

Ghost Employees

Fictitious employees added to payroll receiving salaries that go to fraudsters

Inflated Salaries or Hours

Overstated compensation or falsified working hours draining resources

Excessive Incentives

Unauthorized bonuses and incentive payments siphoning funds
