Advanced Expatriate Payroll Features

Our platform covers all aspects of international employee compensation

01

Dual Currency Payslips

Caters for expatriate payslips with components and allowances in more than one currency, providing clarity for international employees.

02

Home to Host Conversions

Caters for guaranteed net pay in home currency but payable in host currency, ensuring seamless cross-border payments.

03

Gross Up Functionality

Single or Multi Component gross up functionality. Net Pay guarantees for expats require gross ups of net pay in host currency.

04

Tax Equalisation Scenarios

Ensures expats' net remuneration after tax remains unaffected by different tax rates in host and home countries = tax neutral position.
